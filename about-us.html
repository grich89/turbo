About Turbonomic

Turbonomic's Hybrid Cloud Management Platform delivers 30% faster application response time on 30% less infrastructure

Turbonomic: Engineered Disruption of IT

Turbonomic’s Hybrid Cloud Management Platform enables heterogeneous environments to self-manage to assure the performance of any application in any cloud. Turbonomic’s patented decision engine dynamically analyzes application demand and allocates shared resources in real time to maintain a continuous state of application health.

Launched in 2010, Turbonomic is one of the fastest growing technology companies in the virtualization and cloud space. Turbonomic’s Hybrid Cloud Management Platform is trusted by thousands of enterprises to accelerate their adoption of virtual, cloud, and container deployments for all mission critical applications.

Learn more on our product page and solutions pages.

 

Turbonomic: Trusted Autonomic Solution

The Turbonomic platform first launched in August 2010 and now has more than 100,000 users, including many of the world’s leading banks, financial institutions, social and e-commerce sites, carriers and service providers.

Using Turbonomic, our customers, including AT&T, Travelport and Thomson Reuters, assure that applications get the resources they need to operate reliably, while utilizing their most valuable infrastructure and human resources most efficiently. Hear what specific customers have to say or see who’s on board by industry, or visit the Green Circle Community and sign up to join the conversation.

Turbonomic Growth

Through Q3 2016, Turbonomic has experienced 25 consecutive quarters of record revenue growth. On an annual basis we grew revenue at over 100% in 2015 versus 2014. See the latest news and company developments in our Press Room.

People are the difference at Turbonomic – in our success to date, in serving our customers and in achieving the high goals we have as a company. We are constantly looking for the best proven and developing talent to join, build and grow with our high performing team. Whether in sales, engineering, marketing, finance, HR or any other function, if you have the drive and determination, take the first step towards joining us on our careers page.

Turbonomic: Well-funded by Proven Tech Investors

Turbonomic’s technology platform, strategy and ability to achieve the market opportunity before us is further enabled by tech savvy investors who are eager to provide the funding and expert insight to support our aggressive growth goals. At the beginning of 2017, Turbonomic closed $50MM in Series E funding led by General Atlantic, with additional participation from our current investors including: Bain Capital Ventures, Globespan Capital Partners, Highland Capital Partners, and ICONIQ Capital. In addition, we are excited to add a world class IT leader and strategist, Gary Reiner, Operating Partner at General Atlantic and former CIO of General Electric, to our Board of Directors.
